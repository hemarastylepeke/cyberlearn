Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 20, 2025

1. Introduction

At NoteMaster, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our note-taking application and services. Please read this privacy policy carefully. By using our services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Personal Information

  • Name and contact information (email address)
  • Account credentials and authentication data
  • Payment and billing information (for Premium subscriptions)
  • Notes and content you create within the application
  • User preferences and settings
  • Communication history with our support team

2.2. Technical Information

When you use NoteMaster, we may collect technical data including IP addresses, browser types, device information, operating system details, usage patterns, and performance metrics. This helps us improve our service, ensure security, and provide better support.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your NoteMaster account
  • To deliver and improve our note-taking features and services
  • To process payments for Premium subscriptions
  • To provide technical support and customer service
  • To send service-related notifications and updates
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our application
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • To prevent fraud and enhance security

4. Your Notes and Data

Your notes belong to you. We treat all notes and personal content you create as your intellectual property. We will never share, sell, or use your notes for any purpose other than providing you with the NoteMaster service. Your data is stored securely and remains private unless you explicitly choose to share it.

5. Data Protection and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure cloud storage with encrypted backups
  • Multi-factor authentication options
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls limiting data visibility to authorized personnel only
  • Compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other data protection regulations

6. Information Sharing

We keep your information private and do not share it except in these limited circumstances:

  • With essential service providers (hosting, payment processors, email services)
  • When required by law or to comply with legal processes
  • To protect NoteMaster's rights, privacy, or safety
  • With your explicit consent

All third-party providers are carefully selected, contractually bound to maintain data security, and prohibited from using your information for their own purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information and notes at any time
  • Request corrections to your account information
  • Export your notes in a portable format
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications
  • Object to automated decision-making processes

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@notemaster.com.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your user experience, maintain your session, and analyze how you use NoteMaster. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Essential cookies required for platform functionality cannot be disabled, but you may opt-out of analytics and marketing cookies.

9. Data Retention

We retain your account information and notes for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account, we securely delete all associated data within 30 days, unless we're required to retain it for legal or compliance reasons. Payment and billing records are retained for 7 years to comply with tax and accounting regulations.

10. Free vs. Premium Plans

Free Plan: No personal payment information is required. We collect only your email address and account preferences. You can use NoteMaster indefinitely with up to 50 notes at no cost.

Premium Plans: To process your monthly ($7/month) or annual subscription, we collect payment information through secure payment processors. 11. Children's Privacy

NoteMaster is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account promptly.

12. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws than your home country. By using NoteMaster, you consent to such transfers. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place and comply with applicable data protection laws including GDPR for European users.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through prominent notices on our website. Your continued use of NoteMaster following such changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Contact Information

For privacy-related inquiries or to exercise your data rights, please contact us at:

Email: support@notemaster.com

We typically respond to privacy inquiries within 24-48 hours.
