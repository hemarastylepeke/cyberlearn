Our Commitment to You

At NoteMaster (operated by Cyber Learn Limited), we want you to be completely satisfied with your subscription. If you're not happy with our service, we offer a fair and transparent refund policy. Your satisfaction is our priority.

Free Plan

Our Free plan is completely free no credit card required, no hidden charges. You can use it indefinitely with up to 50 notes and basic features.

Cost: $0 forever • Refunds: Not applicable (no charges)

Premium Plans (Monthly & Annual)

14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

All Premium subscriptions (both monthly and annual) come with a 14-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with NoteMaster Premium, you can request a full refund within 14 days of your initial subscription purchase. No questions asked.

  • Applies to first-time Premium subscribers only
  • Full refund of the subscription fee
  • Must be requested within 14 days of purchase
  • Refund processed to original payment method via Paddle
  • Available for both monthly ($7/month) and annual plans

✓ Paddle's Consumer Protection

Your payment is processed securely through Paddle, our merchant of record. This 14-day refund policy meets Paddle's minimum consumer protection requirements, giving you peace of mind with your purchase.

After 14 Days

Subscriptions are non-refundable after the 14-day window. However, you can cancel anytime, and you'll retain access until the end of your current billing period. For annual plans cancelled after 14 days, you'll continue to have access for the remainder of your subscription term.

How to Request a Refund

Requesting a refund is simple and straightforward. Follow these steps:

1

Contact Our Support Team

Email us at support@notemaster.com with the subject line "Refund Request"

2

Provide Required Information

Include your account email address and the reason for your refund request (optional but helpful)

3

Receive Confirmation

We'll respond within 24-48 hours to confirm your refund request

4

Refund Processed

Refunds are processed by Paddle within 5-7 business days to your original payment method

Note: Depending on your bank or payment provider, it may take an additional 3-10 business days for the refund to appear in your account.

Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Subscriptions after 14 days - Refunds are not provided after the 14-day money-back guarantee period has expired.
  • Subscription renewals - Refunds are not provided for automatic renewals. Cancel before your renewal date to avoid charges.
  • Partial periods - Subscriptions cannot be refunded for partial billing periods (except within the 14-day guarantee).
  • Add-on purchases - Any additional features or add-ons purchased separately (if applicable in the future).
  • Accounts suspended for violations - No refunds are provided if your account is terminated due to Terms of Service violations.

Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your NoteMaster Premium subscription at any time through your account settings or by contacting support.

What Happens When You Cancel?

  • You'll retain Premium access until the end of your current billing period
  • No further charges will be made
  • After the period ends, your account will revert to the Free plan
  • Your notes and data remain safe—you'll keep up to 50 notes on the Free plan
  • Cancellations within 14 days of initial purchase are eligible for a full refund

Important: To avoid being charged for the next billing cycle, cancel at least 24 hours before your renewal date.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get a refund if I forgot to cancel before renewal?

Renewal charges are generally non-refundable. However, if you contact us within 48 hours of an accidental renewal, we may be able to process a refund on a case-by-case basis.

How long does it take to receive my refund?

We process refunds through Paddle within 5-7 business days. Depending on your bank, it may take an additional 3-10 days for the funds to appear in your account.

What happens to my notes if I get a refund?

Your notes are safe! After a refund, your account will revert to the Free plan. You can keep up to 50 notes. If you have more, you'll need to delete or export the excess before the downgrade takes effect.

Does the 14-day guarantee apply to both monthly and annual plans?

Yes! Both monthly and annual Premium subscriptions come with the same 14-day money-back guarantee from the date of your initial purchase.

Can I get a prorated refund on my annual plan?

No, prorated refunds are not available after the 14-day guarantee period. However, you can cancel anytime and will continue to have access for the remainder of your subscription term.

Do you offer refunds for technical issues?

If you experience significant technical issues that prevent you from using the service, please contact our support team immediately. We'll work to resolve the issue or discuss refund options if necessary.
